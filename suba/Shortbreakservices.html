Autisk is a constituted support community group offering social groups, workshops, advice and signposting to families who have children with additional needs.

1. The child prefers to play alone rather than with other children.
2. The child finds it difficult to make eye contact with others.
3. The child easily understands when someone is happy or sad.
4. The child gets very upset when their routine is changed.
5. The child enjoys imaginative play (e.g., pretending to be a superhero or playing make-believe).
