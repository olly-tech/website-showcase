ER verbs

Conjugating "-er" verbs in the present tense

"-er" verbs

-er verbs are the most common verb type in French.

When conjugating -er verbs, first cut off ER part and then add the ending.
These are the endings below.

Pronoun Ending
je e
tu es
il/ elle/ on e
nous ons
vous ez
ils/ elles ent

So, jouer would be conjugated like this...

First take off the "er" from jouer

So, it becomes jou

Next, add the appropriate endings.

Pronoun Ending
je joue
tu joues
il/ elle/ on joue
nous jouons
vous jouez
ils/ elles jouent
