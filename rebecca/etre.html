Être

Conjugating "être" in the present tense

"Être" means "to be" in French.

Pronoun Verb
je suis
tu es
il/ elle/ on est
nous sommes
vous êtes
ils/ elles sont

"Être" is an irregular verb but it is a very frquently used. So unfortunately, you have to just suck it up and learn it.

Conjugating "être" in the present tense is the first step to forming the perfect tense for reflexive and MRS VANDERTRAMP verbs in French so if you wish to communicate about your past, (perhaps, how you went to school) you need to learn this.
