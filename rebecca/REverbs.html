RE verbs

Conjugating "-re" in the present tense

"-re" verbs

-re verbs are the least common verb type in French.

When conjugating -re verbs, first cut off -re part and then add the ending.
These are the endings below.

_ this means there is no ending; you just leave the stem as is.
So for the il/elle/on row there is no ending added on the end of the stem.

Pronoun Ending
je s
tu s
il/ elle/ on _
nous ons
vous ez
ils/ elles ent

So, fondre would be conjugated like this...

First take off the "re" from fondre

So, it becomes fond

Next, add the appropriate endings.

Pronoun Ending
je fonds
tu fonds
il/ elle/ on fond
nous fondons
vous fondez
ils/ elles fondent
