Irregular Verbs

Irregular verbs are rule-breakers.
They woke up in the morning, looked out at the world, and chose chaos.
Viva la revolución!

Irregular verbs can stick around continually being rule-breakers because they are among the most commonly used verbs. In the same vein, unfortunately for you young padawans, they are unlikely to never become regular verbs.
So you'll just have to stick around and revise them.

Examples of irregular verbs

French English
Être To be
Aller To go
Avoir To have
Faire To make/ do
Comprendre To understand
Pouvoir To be able to (can)
