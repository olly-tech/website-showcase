Avoir

Conjugating "Avoir" in the present tense

"Avoir" means "to have" in French.

Pronoun Verb
j' ai
tu as
il/ elle/ on a
nous avons
vous avez
ils/ elles ont

REMEMBER in French- when a word ends in a vowel and the next word begins with a vowel- those vowels are removed and an apostrophe is added to combine them into a contraction
For example, "je" + "ai" = "j'ai"

"Avoir" is an irregular verb but it is a very frquently used. So unfortunately, you have to just suck it up and learn it.

Conjugating "Avoir" in the present tense is the first step to forming the perfect tense in French so if you wish to communicate about your past, (perhaps, how you met your best friend) you need to learn this.
