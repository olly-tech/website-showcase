THE ZONE


Welcome to The Zone.
A space for you to tune out the world, learn more about ADHD, and find resources to help you succeed.
Created by a fellow ADHDer, The Zone is a labour of love designed to help you refocus, learn and grow. There are quizzes to discover fun new things about yourself, a void to throw all your worries in to, resources to help you learn more about ADHD, and even a zone out section for when you need a break.
Take a moment to take a deep breath, relax and figure out what you need to do to get back in the zone.
