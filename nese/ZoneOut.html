Guided Meditation

Meditation can help train the brain to improve focus and concentrate better. Mindfulness helps improve the ability to control attention and to develop different relationships to stressful experiences, while also making you more aware of your emotions, which in turn can help aid in being less impulsive!



ADHD Relief Music

Numerous studies have shown music can have a positive impact on the brain, & can help to improve focus,concentration & overall cognitive function. This type of music can be used as background noise while studying, working or engaging in other tasks that require concentration, as it often includes soothing melodies & calming rhythms that relax the mind.



Lo-Fi Music

Music not only provides basic enjoyment, it can also increase dopamine levels, reduce anxiety, enhance focus & strenghten your time awareness! Lo-fi has a repetitive and non-instrusive soundscape, it can help provide a consistent auditory backdrop tasks requiring concentration, without distracting from your main task.
